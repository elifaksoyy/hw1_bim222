Etliekmek is very popular in Konya.This meal has become a symbol of the city and It is made more beautiful than other cities in Konya.If you go Konya,you should certainly try it :) Havzan Etliekmek is the most preferred place.

(3)Gombo Soup

One of Konya's most popular dishes is meaty gombo soup , which is indispensable for weddings and special occasions.You can see it all wedding meals so we can say this is a traditional taste.Dried flower gombos,beef and lemon juice give an original flavor.













(4)Tirit

As it is in many Anatolian cities, tirit also has a beautiful place in Konya Cuisine. This meal takes place at the front of the table at weddings and special occasions.













(5)Arabası Soup

A taste that is as famous as a gumbo soup is a Arabası Soup. Indispensable soup in winter and people prefered to eat with crowded.
